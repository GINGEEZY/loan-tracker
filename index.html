Duration: Interest accrues from the start date and keeps growing until the loan is fully paid. While unpaid, it is calculated up to today.
Interest: (Principal × 10% × full months) + (Principal × 10% × days ÷ 30).
Settled on: Once the full balance is paid, the date of the final payment becomes the end date and interest stops there.
